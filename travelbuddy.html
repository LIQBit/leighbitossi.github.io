Travel Buddy

This is a fantasy football web-based application built in django, that pulls NFL player and game stats from sportsdata.io and also uses JavaScript for various functionalities. Currently, users can register an account, edit their team name, add and delete players to/from their roster, and check their team's weekly score against other users on the "Scoreboard page.
